About

Visual Production

Based in Wolverhampton, I work across the full production pipeline — from planning and on-location camera operation through to editing, colour grading, and final delivery.

Over 12 months working with HashAI I've filmed live events, directed talking-head interviews, and graded raw S-Log footage shot by videographers in the UAE. Alongside that I've produced brand content for Optimise Care and covered automotive events for Yiannimize.
